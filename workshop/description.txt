[h1]Smart Priorities[/h1]
[b]Version 2.2:[/b] Life Support is always High (4) and Toggle is always Very High (5), since these essential categories have no meaningful skill to rank.
[b]Version 2.1:[/b] Manage each duplicant directly from a new checkbox column in the Priorities screen. The header checkbox toggles the whole colony.

Automatically balances duplicant work priorities according to their relative skill levels across the colony.

[b]Features[/b]
[list]
[*]Compares duplicants by the relevant attribute for each work category.
[*]Assigns the highest priority to the most capable duplicants.
[*]Distributes everyone else proportionally between high, normal, and low priority.
[*]Gives equal priority to duplicants with equal skill.
[*]Never assigns priority 0 or 1 to an eligible duplicant, so every work category remains covered.
[*]Keeps everyone at normal priority when all duplicants start at the same level.
[*]Respects work restrictions caused by duplicant traits.
[*]Supports both standard and Bionic duplicants.
[*]Rebalances automatically every 60 seconds of game time.
[*]Can be disabled individually for any duplicant from their side panel.
[*]Per-duplicant choices are stored in the save; automatic management is enabled by default.
[*]Dead or despawning duplicants are safely ignored by recalculation and UI controls.
[/list]
